FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What's included in the membership fee?

All memberships include access to our state-of-the-art equipment, locker rooms, and basic amenities. Higher-tier plans include additional benefits like group classes, personal training sessions, and nutrition planning.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. Contact our membership team for assistance with plan changes.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We offer flexible cancellation with no fees. You can cancel your membership at any time, and it will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes, we offer 15% discounts for students with valid ID and corporate partnerships. Contact our membership team to learn more about eligibility and verification requirements.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. A small monthly maintenance fee applies during the freeze period.
