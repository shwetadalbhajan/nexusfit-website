FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and weekends from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Our staff is available during these hours to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Do you offer free trials?

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial for new members. This includes access to all equipment and one group fitness class. Contact us to schedule your free trial session.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide lockers (bring your own lock), showers, and all necessary equipment.

Do you have personal trainers available?

Absolutely! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. You can book a complimentary consultation to discuss your fitness goals and create a personalized training plan.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample free parking available for all members. Our parking lot is well-lit and monitored by security cameras for your safety.
